How it works

Tell the World your Vision

Describe your Vision of Utopia in some short words. It does not matter, if your Vision is realistic or unrealistic about the economy, society, nature or architecture.

Vote for Visions

To see which Visions are the most valueable: Swipe through the collected Visons and decide which ones you agree with.

Suggest Modifications

You have a similar Vision? Or you would like to add something? Modify a Vision and others will decide which Version to become our Utopia!

Browse in our Utopia!

You want to see our Utopia? Browse through the Visons and see which are the most popular ones!
