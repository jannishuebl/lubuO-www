How it works

Tell your Vision

Describe your Vision of Utopia in some short words. It does not matter if your Vision is realistic, about the economy, society, nature or architecture.

Vote for Visions

To see which Visions are the most valueable: Swipe through the collected Visons and decide with which you agree.

Suggest Modifications

You have a similar Vision? Or you would like to add something? Modify a Vision and others will decide which Version to become our Utopia!

Browse in our Utopia!

You want to see our Utopia? Browse through the Visons and see which are the most popular ones!

Principles

  • Anonyme, no body sees who submitted which Vision.
  • Free, as in Freedom! All Software is released under a Licence that guarantees Freedom.
  • Open, everybody is more then welcome to help and decide on which direction the project goes!
  • More will follow!
